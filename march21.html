Likes:

I need to address my favorite part of “What’s the Use?”: Thundercat’s phenomenal base performance. This song was performed at Mac’s Tiny Desk Concert on youtube and it made me a fan. I like how relaxed Mac’s music is. The lyrics are also very profound as they are in his music and you can get a good idea of the struggles he was facing before his death. “Wings” is my favorite song on the album and I think it has the most meaningful lyrics and melodic beat. There are other songs like “Ladders” which is much more upbeat and optimistic. The album has a great range that reflects Mac’s life and I thoroughly enjoy it time and time again.

Dislikes:

“Conversation Pt. 1” is not my favorite which seems to be a common theme as it has the least number of streams out of all the songs on the album. In a similar way that “NC-17” is not enough to make Astroworld a 9/10, this song is not enough to take away a point from this album. I think it is a beautiful album deserving to be among the ranks of “This Old Dog” and “Astroworld” at the top of my rankings.





Likes:

“What You Need” is a very good vibe that matches my music taste. You can tell that this is older because of the lack of the synth and drums that you can typically hear in his new music. The first half of “House of Balloons / Glass Table Girls” has a really cool beat that I enjoyed. “The Morning” is one of the two songs that I knew from before listening to this, but it is by far my favorite song. “Wicked Games” is also a classic Weeknd song that everyone should listen to. “The Party & The After Party” is a good song that samples the next song I will recommend. The other songs on this album are also very high quality and it builds my respect for The Weeknd.

Dislikes:

My main complaint about this song is the length of the songs. Every other song is almost 7 minutes long which I strongly dislike. Overall I don’t not like this album, but it’s not my favorite. I think that Drake’s “So Far Gone” is a better 10 year release than this. I just don’t find it that special which is why it is going to receive a 6/10.

Likes:

I liked that Kirwin and Mac are friends because it showed Mac in a very comfortable position. I am biased in this review because I already know a lot about him, so I viewed it in a different light than you might. The two talked about interesting things like touring, other individuals in the music industry, and who Mac is as a person. It was a cool new take filmed in his home studio which is iconic for his fans.

Dislikes:

The interview was really great but it made me super jealous. Hearing about his intimate concerts of 50 or less people and how nice of a person he is made me want to experience what they were talking about. It sucks because I don’t know if Mac is going to tour again and he tours following released music, so I have no clue when the next time he’s dropping music. Overall it was a solid interview.
