March 2021

Morning Sex - 8/10

by Ralph Castelli

I don’t know anything about the artist, Ralph Castelli but I do like his songs. I found it through the recommended list at the bottom of my indie spotify playlist. The recommended songs truly separate Spotify from Apple music, and that's indisputable. I hope you enjoy the review!





Likes:

I like the use of the piano synth in this song a lot. The initial intro is nice with some instrument that I don’t know. The vocals and the low pitched piano works really well especially around 1:50. It's a pretty basic song, but I enjoy it and it's only about 3 minutes which is short enough for the relatively straightforward song. Without drums the short length and soothing piano round the song out well.

Dislikes:

Yeah, I do like this song but it is kind of boring. I think that it’s a great song to listen to while you stare at the ceiling or just to relax, but there are other more diverse songs that I prefer. Musically I like how the song fits together, but the lack of drums make it hard for me to stay interested.



Old Friends/Bookends - 8/10

by Simon and Garfunkel

I know this is a recommendation out of left field, but I figured I would share a quality song. My mom introduced me to Simon and Garfunkel and this song is by far my favorite song. I love how music can connect you closer to other people. My friend Andrew had Folk as his number two genre for the year and he gave me tons of more recommendations about music. In the future I’m going to review his playlists.





Likes:

The actual instruments used in this song are absolutely the best part of the song. I like how it feels almost medieval, and the piano and other strings in combination with the harp are really nice together. The climax of the song builds a pressure in my chest around 1:44 and 2:22. I actually prefer the single mix of “Old Friends” and “Bookends” because it allows the song to wind down more.

Dislikes:

I don’t like the build up to the final drop of the song around 2:00 because the strings almost squeal, but it is amazing when it does drop. I think that I really like the harp in the song but I like the climax and drop even more. My waiting takes away from the song but it is still a magnificent song.



Come Down - 6/10

by Anderson .Paak

Anderson .Paak is in my mind just the “YES LAWD” guy, but I definitely associate him to this song. The beat is absolutely contagious and I just want to get into this review.





Likes:

This beat is beyond catchy and I think Paak’s voice is tame for his music. My friends like a lot more of his music but I can find it partially preachy at times, but not in this song. I already added this song to my playlist and think that the beat and fast paced drums and guitar are amazing.

Dislikes:

I don’t have many complaints about this song with the exception of its length. The beat doesn’t change throughout the course of the song which is a negative trend that I often mention. Otherwise I really enjoy this song, although it doesn’t excite me as much as others do which is why I rated this an average score.
