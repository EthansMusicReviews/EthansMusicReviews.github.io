May 2021


Likes:

The beat is super ominous and reminds me of “5% Tint” by Travis Scott. The bars that Critch delivers are out of this world. I think the 808’s are placed very well. Critch’s voice works really well with the beat and the song definitely makes me bop my head to the beat.

Dislikes:

My main complaint about this song is that the 808’s hit twice which sounds off when you listen on headphones, but sounds good on car speakers. It gives the weird effect of sounding like bass slap, but it does what it is intended to do.





Likes:

The core beat of this song definitely gets my head bopping. Originally leaked on Tik Tok, this song did not disappoint when the full song was released. The synth and drums on the song flow together really well. The chorus is also super catchy and variations of the speed of the delivery of vocals make this song a hit.

Dislikes:

I don’t have any complaints as of now, but I feel like this is a song that could get old fast and there is a chance it withstands the test of time. I guess we just have to see what happens.





Likes:

I think that the airy synths and beat make this song incredibly happy. Even though the song is repetitive, the beat is so entertaining. The vocals create a feeling in your chest that makes you want to drive with the windows down on a summer night at the beach. It makes me want to get up and dance.

Dislikes:

I’m not sure if I have any dislikes, but I don’t think I can give it a 10 quite yet. I feel like it is so common for teenagers to listen to sad music, but happy music is something that is unfamiliar. Maybe in the future if I can relax more this song will be a 10, but for now I’m not even sure what to rate it.





Likes:

This live was pretty much on par with Rex’s old lives, but the new song was phenomenal. It alone added points to this review and created so much hype for his new album. Likes:

The flow on this song is out of this world. The beat and intro are phenomenal. I think that J Cole is hitting a new level in his rap which is a shame because he was just signed to a basketball team in Africa. Overall this song makes me really excited for the album dropping in two days. The pauses in the beat and the overall drums fit so well with the song.

Dislikes:

I think that the intro to this song is a little bit too long seeing as though the vocals carry the song. I can’t criticize his vocals because they are perfect and absolutely fire. However, I am hesitant to give this song a 10 because I haven’t heard the album yet and want to view it in the frame of the entire album before I can rank it at the top of the list.





Likes:

The beat on this song goes perfectly with the high pitched synth of Don’s vocals. The hit of the 808’s and drums in combination with the emphasis of the vocals. The overall flow of the song makes you jump around in your seat. This is probably one of my favorite Don songs he’s ever released.

Dislikes:

My only complaint is that the song is a little repetitive which is the result of a long chorus. I would like to hear Don spit on a verse rather than make a more pop based song.
