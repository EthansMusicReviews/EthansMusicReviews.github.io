April 2021


Likes:

I like that the beat and the female vocals sound very melodic and Tyler-like. It sounds like it is sampled from the album “Flower Boy”, but I’m not sure. I like the bars and think that there is a ton of potential, but this song just isn’t too phenomenal. I think that the main attraction to this song is the beat, and not the vocals which is concerning for his future as an artist.

Dislikes:

I know that as a new artist, Xavier likely doesn’t have the quality recording equipment that so many artists use and that I am used to, but his sounds really bad. I feel like the mixing made the vocals way too loud over the beat during his verses. His voice also whines rather than drags when he sings during the chorus which I find to be annoying.





Likes:

Maybe the main fingerpicking rift for the past two songs was too similar, but I feel like this was a new sound that Will didn’t complicate or ruin with other instruments. He kept it raw with angelic harmonies and soft vocals. I have been waiting for him to release and this didn’t disappoint. I like the regular version, but the striped one in the video above is beyond perfect.

Dislikes:

I really can’t think of any complaints about this song. I think that it deserves the 10/10 because he kept it stripped. I have listened to it on loop, and it doesn’t get old. I think this is exactly what I hoped from the demo video on Tik Tok and I am ecstatic about this song.
