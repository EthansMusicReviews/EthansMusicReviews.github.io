Welcome

This is the reviews page! Here I will review the events of the week and discuss my methods for finding new music.

Chandelier 7/10

By Will Paquin

Paquin began his music career on tik tok when he posted a video sitting on a porch with a sunset in the background. I remember scrolling on my for you page and feeling an unusual comfort when he played his first draft of his first song "Chandelier". I like the song and listen all the way through each time, but I enjoyed the stripped down version better with just vocals

Talkhouse Podcast - 6/10

The talkhouse podcast is a true treat for any indie lover. Instagram ads blessed me and advertised that Mac Demarco, one of my favorite artists, had a podcast with Dayglow an artist that I knew of but not about. In the podcast, the two artists discuss touring, the impact of COVID on the industry, and various other topics. Demarco becomes a big brother type of figure during the podcast by imparting tidbits of information with young Dayglow yet they also can share a laugh over their work. I enjoyed it, but found it to be a little slow at times, but that is what comes with Indie artists and it is always nice to slow down in the bustle and hustle of every day.

Savage Mode 2 - 2/10

Metro Boomin and 21 Savage released their much anticipated follow up to the iconic album "Savage Mode", and I was sadly underwhelmed. Their first album yielded songs that are still on my playlist nearly four and a half years later like "X" and "Savage Mode". In their second album, I found the intro "Intro" featuring Morgan Freedman to be the best part of the album. The track "Mr. Right Now" featuring Drake has a weird high pitched beat mixed with 808's and hi-hats. In my opinion without the main beat, it would have been a hit. Overall disappointed and skipped most of the songs in the album.
