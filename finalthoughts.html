April 2021

This project has been both trying and enjoyable. I had to change the color theme, navigation structure, and page structure multiple times. However, one year of reviewing music has not made me hate music. I still love it, but I think that I can better analyze why I like what I’m listening to. Now I focus on specific sounds and am more picky with what I like. I’m planning on editing my playlists this summer and exploring the jazz genre more in depth. My taste has shifted away from Indie this year and towards rap but I think it may be a seasonal change. I never thought I would make this website, but I sure am glad that I did and that I could share it with the few who view it. Keep listening - Ethan
